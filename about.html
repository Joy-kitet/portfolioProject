About Me

Hello! I'm DebraJoy, I'm passionate about technology, especially mobile app development and fitness tracking solutions. I love building user-friendly applications that enhance people's lives, particularly in health and wellness. Currently, I'm focused on integrating Health Connect into my fitness tracker app to help users monitor their calorie intake and overall progress. I enjoy working with Flutter and Jetpack Compose, always striving to improve my skills and stay updated with the latest tech trends. My goal is to create seamless, data-driven applications that provide valuable insights to users while maintaining a great user experience. Beyond coding, I'm excited about how technology can bridge gaps in health, fitness, and productivity. I believe that smart integrations and thoughtful design can make a real impact on people's daily routines.
